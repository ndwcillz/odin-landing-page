Joyce Kilmer Slickrock Wilderness

Experience the untouched marvels of the Joyce Kilmer Slickrock Wilderness. Straddling the ancient ridges of North Carolina and Tennessee, this 17,000-acre escape offers secluded backcountry trails and access to one of the largest tracts of pristine old-growth forest in the Eastern United States.

Experience the Beauty of the Wilderness.

Experience Views of Lake Santeetlah from the Haoe Lead Trail.

Experience Stunning Visuals on the top of Hangover allowing you to see all nearby cities.

Experience Fast Flowing Rivers and the Soothing Sound as you Sleep!
